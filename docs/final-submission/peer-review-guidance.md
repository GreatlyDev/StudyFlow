## Suggested Comment Style

Keep comments professional and honest. Good comments mention specific behavior:

- "Kept the team updated and helped organize project work."
- "Contributed to development and testing tasks."
- "Participated in meetings and helped with planning."
- "Helped with documentation and presentation preparation."
- "Could improve by communicating progress earlier."

Avoid personal attacks. Focus on project contribution and teamwork.
